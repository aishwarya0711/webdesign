Frequently asked questions

  • Netflix is an American subscription video on-demand over-the-top streaming service. The service primarily distributes original and acquired films and television shows from various genres, and it is available internationally in multiple languages.

  • The Netflix Standard Subscription Plan costs Rs 499 per month and allows users to stream on up to two devices, including phone, tablet, computer and smart TV, at a time in Full High definition (Full HD) 1080p quality.

  • Netflix does not offer free trials, but you have the freedom to change your plan or cancel online at any time if you decide Netflix isn't for you. There are no contracts, no cancellation fees, and no commitments. You can sign up and take advantage of all Netflix has to offer.

  • Generally, Netflix does not offer refunds for any subscriptions that have already been paid for, regardless of whether you have watched the content or not. However, if you believe that you have been charged in error, you can reach out to Netflix's customer service for assistance.

  • Netflix is a subscription-based streaming service that allows our members to watch TV shows and movies on an internet-connected device. Depending on your plan, you can also download TV shows and movies to your iOS, Android, or Windows 10 device and watch without an internet connection.

  • Netflix “Just for Kids” offers a separate hub of movies and TV shows for under 12s, as part of the normal monthly subscription. The content has all been checked to ensure it's appropriate for under 12s and is separated by a tab called “Just for Kids”.
